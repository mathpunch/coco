Coco the Guinea Pig

Meet the calm, cuddly queen of hideouts

Hi, I'm Coco! I'm a girl guinea pig adopted from PetSmart.
I love snuggling in blankets, hiding in my cozy spots, and munching snacks when I feel safe.
My favorite thing? Hanging out with my human while wrapped in a warm blanket!

About Coco

  • Loves to hide in her favorite spots
  • Enjoys eating treats when out of her hideout
  • Sometimes bites when taken out of her cage or hand-fed (she’s got boundaries!)
  • Came from PetSmart
  • Calm and relaxed most of the day
  • Likes to be held with a blanket wrapped around her

Fun Facts

  • Coco’s favorite snack: fresh parsley!
  • She’ll only come out when she feels safe
  • Blankets make her feel extra cozy
  • She has a gentle side, but watch your fingers!
